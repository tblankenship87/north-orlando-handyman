Common Questions

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Seminole County, FL — Sanford, Lake Mary, Longwood, Altamonte Springs, Oviedo, Winter Springs, and surrounding areas.

Do I need to provide parts?

For most flat-rate services, yes — you provide the fixture or part, we provide the labor. This keeps costs lower and lets you pick exactly what you want.

How soon can you come out?

Most jobs are available same-week. Once you submit your request, we'll confirm availability and lock in your appointment within a few hours.

Is your work guaranteed?

Yes — every job comes with a 1-year workmanship guarantee. If something isn't right, we come back and fix it. No questions asked.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and check. Payment is due on completion of the job.
